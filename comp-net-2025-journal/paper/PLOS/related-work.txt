This manuscript extends a published conference paper: Towell, A., Matta, J. (2025). Cognitive MRI of AI Conversations: Complex Network Analysis of ChatGPT Conversation Archives. In: Complex Networks & Their Applications XIII. Studies in Computational Intelligence, vol 1189. Springer, Cham. The conference paper analyzed the static network structure of a ChatGPT conversation archive. The present manuscript adds temporal evolution analysis (densification laws, preferential attachment, community lifecycle tracking, bridge dynamics, model era effects), constituting approximately 60% new material. A copy of the conference paper is uploaded as a Related Manuscript. All figures are original to this submission.
