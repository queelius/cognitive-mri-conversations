DATA AVAILABILITY STATEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------
The conversation embedding dataset and analysis code are available at
https://github.com/queelius/chatgpt-complex-net (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.15314235).
Raw conversation content is not shared to protect the privacy of the human
participant, but all derived data (embeddings, edges, temporal metrics) needed
to reproduce the analyses are included.

------------------------------------------------------------
COVER LETTER KEY POINTS
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Why suitable for PLOS Complex Systems:
   - Applies established complex network analysis (densification, preferential
     attachment, community dynamics) to a novel domain (AI conversation archives)
   - Empirical case study with reproducible methodology and open data
   - Bridges temporal network theory and human-AI interaction research

2. How it will inspire the field:
   - Demonstrates that individual AI-assisted knowledge exploration follows the
     same macroscopic laws as collective knowledge systems
   - Introduces "cognitive MRI" as a replicable framework for analyzing
     conversation archives from any LLM platform
   - Provides practical implications for knowledge management tool design
