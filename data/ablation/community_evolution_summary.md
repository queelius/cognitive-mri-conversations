## Key Insights

### User-Heavy Ratios (100:1, 4:1, 2:1)
- Communities organized around **user interests and questions**
- Clear separation between technical domains
- Higher topic purity within communities

### Balanced Ratio (1:1)
- Communities blend user questions with AI elaborations
- More cross-domain communities emerge
- Moderate topic coherence

### AI-Heavy Ratios (1:2, 1:4, 1:100)
- Communities dominated by **AI response patterns**
- Technical topics merge into broader clusters
- Lower topic purity, more generic groupings
